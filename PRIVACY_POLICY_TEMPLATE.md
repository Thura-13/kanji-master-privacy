# Privacy Policy for Kanji Master

**Last Updated: November 6, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Kanji Master ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Kanji Master stores the following data locally on your device:
- **Learning Progress**: Your kanji learning progress, quiz scores, and statistics
- **Preferences**: App settings, theme preferences, and customization choices
- **Study Data**: Bookmarked kanji, review schedules, and mistake tracking
- **Achievements**: Unlocked achievements and progress milestones

**Important**: This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:
- ❌ Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Photos or media files
- ❌ Contact lists
- ❌ Device identifiers for tracking purposes

## How We Use Your Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:
- ✅ Track your learning progress
- ✅ Provide personalized review schedules
- ✅ Remember your app preferences
- ✅ Show your achievements and statistics
- ✅ Improve your learning experience

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device information (device type, OS version)
- Advertising ID
- IP address
- App usage data

**AdMob Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Your Choices**:
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- Reset your advertising ID in device settings

### Google Gemini AI (AI Tutor Feature)

When you use the AI Tutor feature:
- Your questions are sent to Google Gemini API for processing
- Conversations are NOT stored by us
- Google may process data according to their privacy policy

**Google AI Privacy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**What We Send**:
- Only your kanji-related questions
- Basic context for better answers

**What We DON'T Send**:
- Personal information
- Device information
- Other app data

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your learning data is stored locally on your device
- Data is not backed up to cloud servers
- Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

### Security Measures
- We use industry-standard encryption for local data storage (Capacitor Preferences API)
- No data transmission to external servers (except AI Tutor queries)
- Regular security updates

## Children's Privacy

Kanji Master is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect personal information, our app is compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- ✅ **Access**: View all your locally stored data within the app
- ✅ **Delete**: Clear your data at any time through app settings or by uninstalling
- ✅ **Control**: Manage theme unlocks and customization
- ✅ **Opt-Out**: Disable personalized ads in device settings

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **AI Tutor Queries**: Not stored by us; processed by Google according to their policy
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics may be retained by AdMob

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

**Email**: nyeinthura57@example.com  
**App**: Use the "Contact Us" feature in More > Help & Support

## Permissions Explained

### Why We Request Certain Permissions

**Internet Access** (android.permission.INTERNET)
- Required for: AI Tutor feature, loading ads
- Optional: App works offline for core features

**Network State** (android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)
- Required for: Checking if internet is available before AI/ad requests
- Privacy impact: None (only checks connection status)

**No Other Permissions Required**
- We do not request access to: Camera, Microphone, Location, Contacts, Storage, etc.

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- ✅ GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- ✅ CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- ✅ COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- ✅ Google Play Store Policies
- ✅ Japanese Privacy Laws (APPI)
